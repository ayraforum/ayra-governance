



## Fee Schedule


| **Member Class** | **Annual Fee** | **Payment Terms** | **Notes** |
|--------------|-----------|---------------|-------|
| **Strategic Member** | <1,000 employees: US$50,000 / >=1,000 employees: US$200,000/yr  | Annual, in full | Large established orgs with major commitment |
| **Associate Member** | <1,000 employees: US$12,500 / >=1,000 employees: US$50,000/yr | Annual, in full | Organizations that want to participate in various Ayra  |
| **Individual Member** | **No fee** | N/A | All Individual Members participate on a non-commercial basis without fees (see note below)] |
| **Ecosystem Member** | **No fee** | N/A | Digital trust ecosystem operators |
| **Civil Society Member** | **No fee** | N/A | Non-profits focused on digital rights/privacy |
| **Government Member** | **No fee** | N/A | Public sector entities |

---





Notes:
### Payment Terms
  - **Fee-based classes (Associate, Strategic):** Payment due upon execution of membership agreement
  - **Membership becomes effective:** Upon payment receipt
  - **Payment window:** 30 days from approval notification
  - **Annual renewal:** Invoice sent 30 days before renewal date

### Individual Members (Non-Commercial)\

**Individual Members** represent non-commercial interests. If an individual is a member of a commercial organization, or an independent business, their efforts should be limited to providing non-commercial input.
